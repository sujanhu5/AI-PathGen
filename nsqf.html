NSQF Levels & Description Table

NSQF Level Descriptive Skill Set Example Mapping
Level 4 Factual knowledge, routine and repetitive tasks with little judgement. Junior Web Assistant, Data Entry Operator, Basic Retail Associate
Level 5 A wide range of skilled work, involving choice of procedures in a variety of contexts. Entry-level Developer, Field Technician, Specialized Service Provider
Level 6 Knowledge of facts, principles, processes and general concepts, self-management. Junior Full Stack Developer (Your Target), Senior Technician, Team Leader
Level 7 Comprehensive, factual and theoretical knowledge in a broad context. Mid-level Developer, Marketing Manager, Master Craftsman
