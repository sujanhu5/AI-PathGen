About AI PathGen

AI PathGen is built on a simple philosophy: **Learning should be personal, strategic, and efficient.** We leverage advanced machine learning models to synthesize data from thousands of job roles, educational content, and accreditation standards (including NSQF) to generate a dynamic, step-by-step roadmap tailored to your unique profile.

Forget generic course suggestions. We provide a structured path that includes the right mix of courses, hands-on projects, soft skill development, and certifications, all aligned with industry demands and your target career frame.
